# Book Identity

## Core Information

**Book Number:** 071
**Title:** Body Swap
**Slug:** body-swap
**Category:** Supernatural/Bizarre

## The Death (One Sentence)
A skeptic who volunteers for a "harmless" body swap ritual at a mystic hostel finds herself trapped in the dying body of a terminally ill elderly woman, unable to return to her own healthy form before it expires.

## Tagline
"Be careful what body you wish for."

## Themes
1. Identity and consciousness - what makes us "us" when separated from our physical form
2. Ageism and how society discards the elderly
3. The danger of treating other cultures' spiritual practices as tourist entertainment

## Tone Balance
- Comedy: 35%
- Horror: 45%
- Satire: 20%

## Target Reader
Travelers who have participated in "authentic" spiritual ceremonies abroad without fully understanding what they were getting into. Anyone who has treated sacred rituals as Instagram content.

## Comparison Titles
- "Get Out" meets "Freaky Friday" with a backpacker twist
- "Midsommar" meets "The Skeleton Key"

## Content Warnings
- Body horror (experiencing physical decay)
- Ageism and elderly suffering
- Terminal illness
- Cultural appropriation of spiritual practices
- Existential horror
- Death by organ failure
